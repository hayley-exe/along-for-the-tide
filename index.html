Along For The Tide


The Story

When life gives you lemons… make lemonade. Due to a sudden need for relocation mid-pandemic (a 75 foot tree landed in our house), we ended up living in a beach house for 6 months and making this the best lemonade ever. Every day I would walk the quiet beaches and collect beautiful shells and sea glass. I have made these jewelry dishes from the shells I found. I keep one on my night table and put my jewelry in it. A daily reminder of the beauty found in tough times. May you always find the good in the bad, the positive in the negative, the treasure in the ocean.
